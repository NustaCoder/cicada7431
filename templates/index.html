Welcome to the Cicada 7431 Challenge!!!


Cicada 7431 is a nickname given to this Challenge that has posted a set of puzzles to be solved by the ardent students . The first internet puzzle from Coding Club Svit. The name of the challenge is derived from the Cicada 3301. Some have claimed that Cicada 3301 is a secret society with the goal of improving cryptography, privacy and anonymity. In Cicada 7431 the intent is to develop "intelligent individuals" by presenting a series of puzzles.

The puzzles are all focused on your problem solving skills. You can always solve the puzzle by simple pen and paper or let the computer do it for you...The choice is yours!!!
